The Enigmatic Echidna

Coming soon - April 2026 Uncover the secrets of the world's most curious creature. Egg-laying, ant-eating and spiky, echidnas are often depicted as weird, strange, primitive and ancient. The real story is very different. Discover the remarkable world of echidnas and find out just how much these spiy, mild-mannered monotremes have to teach us about what makes a mammal.

Koala

Despite their iconic status and celebrity, koalas remain something of a mystery. Delve into the world of koalas to discover what’s behind the sweet face on thousands of postcards. From their megafaunal ancestors to the devastating bushfires of 2019–2020, this is an immersive and entertaining story of the hidden life of koalas and their complex relationship with humans and trees.

In Search of the Woman who Sailed the World

When the first woman to circumnavigate the world returned home in 1776, she did so without any fanfare at all. For over two centuries the story of who this remarkable young woman was has been shrouded in mystery. The true story is even more surprising than anyone had imagined. An ode to the sea, to science and to one remarkable woman who charted her own course for others to follow.
