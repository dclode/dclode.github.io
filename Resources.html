Inland Sea Teaching Guide

Prehistoric marine life in Australia’s inland sea connects with many aspects of the Australian curriculum, particularly in English and the Biological Science. It can also be used in an interdisciplinary way to support other disciplines including earth sciences, mathematics, art and technology. The underlying theme of climate change, ecological change and extinction underpins the cross-curricular theme of sustainability. The study of Australian bioregions also supports a discussion of Australia’s biological engagement with Asia. Suitable for Foundation to Year 10
